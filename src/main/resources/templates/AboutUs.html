Welcome to Moffat Bay Lodge

Nestled in the tranquil embrace of Joviedsa Island, Moffat Bay Lodge offers an unparalleled retreat for those seeking to escape the everyday hustle. Whether you’re looking to reconnect with nature, embark on exciting outdoor adventures, or simply unwind by the lakeside, our lodge promises a rejuvenating experience like no other. Wake up to the serene sounds of nature, breathe in the crisp mountain air, and immerse yourself in the breathtaking views that surround our picturesque location. At Moffat Bay Lodge, we don’t just provide accommodation – we create lasting memories and experiences that leave you feeling revitalized and inspired.
